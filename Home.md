# Investing Vault — Home

This vault is for **long-term investing only**. Separate from FredLoad business.

## Folders

- **Learning/** — notes from books, courses, concepts I'm studying
- **Watchlist/** — stocks/funds I'm watching or considering
- **Research/** — deep dives on specific companies or funds before buying
- **Decisions/** — log of every buy/sell, why I did it, what I expected
- **Preferences/** — my investing rules, risk tolerance, goals

## Rules for this vault

1. No trading, no day-trading, no forex — long-term only
2. Every buy/sell gets logged in Decisions/ with a date and reason
3. Never invest money I might need within 3 years
4. Emergency fund comes before investing
